Peanut Butter and Chocolate sandwich!

Description

Fast, easy and tasty! Peanut butter sandwich with Chocolate spread.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Chocolate spread
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Get a thin layer of peanut butter on the bread.
  2. Spread the chocolate on the other side of the bread.
  3. Mash them together, and dunk it in a cup of coffee!
